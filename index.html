Welcome To Our Legacy

As All Crazy Everything Entertainment, we have created a competition for hip hop artist to compete with each other by recording a video and posting it on our online facebook page "Adom + a Cypher". Competition runs from February to November, then 10 artists will be chosen to compete in the finals in december, where prizes will be won by number 1, 2 and 3.
